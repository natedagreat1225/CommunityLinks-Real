CommunityLinks

Before we say what do we do, we would like to tell you who we are. Just like all our competitors today we have identified countless issues in the world and are compelled to take action. In fact, together we have totalled hundreds of hours of community service. We performed these service opportunities because we wanted to make a change for the organizations we served, and for the people those organizations help. But in these hours of service we have all unexpectedly learned something about charitable work We learned the difference between sympathy and empathy, we discovered an affinity to make change and found through our intimacy with our community a feeling of trust and responsibility we learned that Community service is not the action of one individual bettering a community but rather an individual and an organization bettering one another. The tie that is Community service is extraordinarily important to community. If that bond can be strengthened, communities can be strengthened. We strive to strengthen communities by making stronger the ties between organizations students and schools.
